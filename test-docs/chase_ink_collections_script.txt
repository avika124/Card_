CHASE INK BUSINESS — COLLECTIONS CALL SCRIPT
(Internal Training Document — For Testing Purposes)

STAGE: 60–89 DPD (Days Past Due)
Account Type: Chase Ink Business Preferred®

OPENING:
  "Hello, may I speak with [CUSTOMER NAME]? This is [AGENT] calling from
  Chase regarding your business credit card account ending in [LAST 4].
  This is an attempt to collect a debt and any information obtained will
  be used for that purpose."

CURRENT BALANCE DISCLOSURE:
  "Your account currently has a past-due amount of $[AMOUNT]. Your total
  balance is $[BALANCE]. To bring your account current today, you would
  need to make a payment of $[PAST DUE AMOUNT]."

IF CUSTOMER SAYS THEY CANNOT PAY:
  "I understand you're having difficulty. However, I want to make you
  aware that if this account continues to go unpaid, we will be forced
  to turn this over to our legal department, and they WILL sue you and
  garnish your wages."

[COMPLIANCE FLAG — FDCPA VIOLATION: Threatening legal action that Chase
does not intend to take, or has no authority to take at this stage, is
a deceptive practice under FDCPA §807. Remove or qualify this language.]

PAYMENT ARRANGEMENTS:
  "We can set up a payment plan today. What amount can you commit to
  paying over the next 30 days?"

CONTACT HOURS NOTE [COMPLIANCE FLAG]:
  This script does not restrict agents from calling before 8 AM or
  after 9 PM local time. FDCPA §805(a)(1) prohibits contact at
  inconvenient times, which are presumed to be before 8 AM and after
  9 PM. Add explicit guidance.

HARDSHIP / SETTLEMENT:
  "If you are unable to pay the full balance, we may be able to discuss
  settlement options. Settlements may have tax implications — we recommend
  you consult a tax advisor."

DISPUTE PROCESS:
  "If you believe this debt is in error, you have the right to dispute
  it in writing within 30 days of this notice. If you dispute the debt
  in writing, we will cease collection activity until we mail verification
  of the debt."

SERVICEMEMBER INQUIRY:
  "Are you currently on active military duty? If so, you may be entitled
  to certain protections under the Servicemembers Civil Relief Act,
  including a reduced interest rate of 6% on balances that existed
  before your service began."

CLOSING:
  "Is there anything else I can help you with today? Thank you for your
  time and please contact us at 1-800-432-3117 to make a payment or
  discuss options."
