INTEREST RATES AND INTEREST CHARGES

Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for Purchases
  0% Introductory APR for the first 15 months from account opening.
  After that, 19.49%–28.24% variable APR based on your creditworthiness
  and the Prime Rate.

APR for Balance Transfers
  0% Introductory APR for the first 15 months from account opening.
  After that, 19.49%–28.24% variable APR.

APR for Cash Advances
  29.99% variable APR.

Penalty APR and When It Applies
  Up to 29.99% variable APR. This APR may be applied to your account
  if you make a late payment. If the Penalty APR is applied, it may apply
  indefinitely.

Paying Interest
  Your due date is at least 21 days after the close of each billing cycle.
  We will not charge you interest on purchases if you pay your entire
  balance by the due date each month. We will begin charging interest on
  cash advances and balance transfers from the date of the transaction.

FEES

Annual Fee: None.

Transaction Fees
  Balance Transfer: Either $5 or 3% of the amount of each transfer,
    whichever is greater.
  Cash Advance: Either $10 or 5% of the amount of each transaction,
    whichever is greater.
  Foreign Transaction: 3% of each transaction in U.S. dollars.

Penalty Fees
  Late Payment: Up to $40.
  Returned Payment: Up to $40.

How We Will Calculate Your Balance:
  We use a method called "average daily balance (including new purchases)."

Loss of Introductory APR:
  We may end your introductory APR early and apply the Penalty APR if you
  make a late payment.

AGREEMENT TERMS

Acceptance
  This Cardmember Agreement ("Agreement") governs your credit card account.
  You promise to pay for all transactions, interest charges, and fees. This
  Agreement is binding when you use the account or make a payment.

Credit Limit
  We will tell you your credit limit. You must not exceed your credit limit.
  We may approve transactions over your credit limit at our discretion but
  this does not obligate us to do so in the future.

Minimum Payment
  Your minimum payment will be the greater of: (a) $25; or (b) 1% of your
  new balance, plus interest and fees. If your balance is less than $25,
  your minimum payment is the full balance.

Cash Advances
  We may limit the amount of cash advances to a portion of your credit limit.
  Cash advances are not eligible for any grace period.

Arbitration Agreement
  PLEASE READ THIS SECTION CAREFULLY. IT AFFECTS YOUR LEGAL RIGHTS.
  
  Agreement to Arbitrate: Either you or we may, without the other's consent,
  elect mandatory, binding arbitration for any claim, dispute, or controversy
  between you and us.
  
  Class Action Waiver: IF ARBITRATION IS CHOSEN BY ANY PARTY, NEITHER YOU
  NOR WE WILL HAVE THE RIGHT TO LITIGATE THAT CLAIM IN COURT OR TO HAVE A
  JURY TRIAL. DISCOVERY AND APPEAL RIGHTS ARE LIMITED IN ARBITRATION.
  NEITHER YOU NOR WE WILL HAVE THE RIGHT TO PARTICIPATE AS A CLASS
  REPRESENTATIVE OR CLASS MEMBER ON ANY CLASS CLAIM.
  
  You have the right to reject this agreement to arbitrate within 60 days
  of account opening by calling the number on the back of your card.

Billing Disputes
  If you think there is an error on your statement, write to us at the
  address on your statement. In your letter, provide your name and account
  number, the dollar amount of the suspected error, and a description of
  the error. You must contact us within 60 days of the first statement
  on which the error appeared.
  
  We must respond within 30 days and resolve the dispute within
  two billing cycles (but no later than 90 days) after we receive
  your letter. While we investigate, you do not have to pay the disputed
  amount or any related interest.

Servicemembers
  If you are covered by the Servicemembers Civil Relief Act (SCRA), you
  may be entitled to a reduced interest rate of 6% on balances incurred
  before your military service. You must submit a written request and a
  copy of your military orders. Contact us at the number on the back of
  your card.

Credit Reporting
  We may report information about your account to credit bureaus. Late
  payments, missed payments, or other defaults on your account may be
  reflected in your credit report.

Governing Law
  Federal law and the law of Ohio govern this Agreement, to the extent
  state law applies.

How to Avoid Paying Interest
  Your due date is at least 21 days after the close of each billing cycle.
  We will not charge you interest on purchases if you pay your entire
  balance by the due date each month.
