CHASE ULTIMATE REWARDS® PROGRAM AGREEMENT
Chase Sapphire Reserve® — Rewards Program Terms
Effective January 1, 2025

1. EARNING POINTS
   You earn points on net purchases (purchases minus returns or refunds)
   charged to your account. Points are earned at the following rates:
   • 10x points on hotels and car rentals through Chase Travel℠
   • 10x points on Chase Dining℠ purchases
   • 10x points on Lyft purchases (through September 30, 2027)
   • 5x points on flights through Chase Travel℠
   • 3x points on other travel and dining not purchased through Chase Travel
   • 1x point on all other purchases

2. POINT VALUE
   Points are worth 1.5 cents each when redeemed for travel through
   Chase Travel℠. We reserve the right to change point values at any
   time, for any reason, with or without notice.

   [UDAAP FLAG: Unilateral devaluation without notice may constitute
   an unfair or deceptive practice if customers were led to believe
   in a fixed point value. Recommend adding minimum notice period.]

3. POINT EXPIRATION
   Points do not expire as long as your account is open and in good
   standing. However, if your account is closed for any reason —
   including at your request — ALL unredeemed points will be forfeited
   immediately with no option for redemption or cash equivalent.

   [UDAAP FLAG: Forfeiture of earned rewards on voluntary closure,
   without adequate notice or grace period, has been cited by CFPB
   as a potential unfair practice. Recommend 90-day redemption window
   post-closure.]

4. POINT TRANSFERS
   You can transfer points to participating airline and hotel partners
   at a 1:1 ratio. Transfer partners and ratios are subject to change
   at any time without notice.

5. ANNUAL TRAVEL CREDIT
   Each account anniversary year, you will receive up to a $300 statement
   credit for travel purchases. "Travel" is defined at our sole discretion
   and the definition may change without notice. Credits are applied
   automatically but may take up to 1–2 billing cycles to appear.

6. PROGRAM CHANGES
   We reserve the right to change, limit, modify, or cancel the rewards
   program at any time. Changes will be announced at our sole discretion.
   Continued use of the card after changes constitutes acceptance of
   the new terms.

7. REDEMPTION OPTIONS
   Points may be redeemed for:
   • Travel through Chase Travel℠ (1.5 cents per point)
   • Cash back as statement credit (1 cent per point)
   • Gift cards (varies by retailer)
   • Pay Yourself Back℠ categories (1.5 cents per point, subject to change)
   • Amazon purchases (0.8 cents per point — reduced value, not prominently disclosed)

   [UDAAP/TILA FLAG: The reduced redemption rate for Amazon (0.8 cents)
   is not prominently disclosed and appears only in footnote #12 of the
   full terms. This disparity should be disclosed at point of enrollment
   and at time of redemption selection to avoid deceptive presentation.]

8. AUTHORIZED USERS
   Primary cardmembers may add authorized users. Authorized users earn
   points that accrue to the primary cardmember's account. If the
   primary cardmember's account is closed, authorized user points
   are also forfeited.

Annual Fee: $550. Global Entry/TSA PreCheck fee credit: up to $120 every
4 years. Priority Pass Select lounge access: unlimited visits for primary
cardholder and two guests.
